What We Do ?

Calicut Garbage Collection is a waste management technology startup that came about in 2024. The idea was simple-using technology to ensure zero waste, a greener earth and a sustainable future for all. Our vision is clear : to become the ultimate destination for the disposal of all types of waste, making it easier for everyone to contribute to a cleaner, greener future. Our aim is to make a sustainable eco-friendly future, reduce environmental impact, and enhance resource efficiency.
